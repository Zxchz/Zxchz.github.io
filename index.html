I teach machines to listen, then take them apart to see how they hear.

Machine learning research — audio foundation models

I work on audio foundation models — the neural networks that turn sound into understanding. Most of my time goes to interpretability: opening these models up, finding the few neurons that actually carry the signal, and cutting away the rest.

I like problems wedged between hardware and theory. I’m still in high school.
