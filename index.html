I take neural networks apart to figure out what they’re actually doing. Lately that means audio models. I also build hardware that probably shouldn’t exist.

// about

I’m sixteen and mostly obsessed with what’s going on inside neural networks — which units fire, which representations actually matter, and how much you can rip out before the whole thing stops working.

Right now that’s audio foundation models: interpretability and structured sparsity, probing internal activations to find the signal hiding under the noise. A couple of writeups are under review at workshops — results pending.

When I’m not doing that I’m building hardware, usually things with no practical purpose whatsoever. It’s more fun than it sounds.
